Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Click Swap?

Click Swap is a Nigerian tap-to-earn platform where users earn coins by tapping, referring others, and performing simple tasks. These coins can be converted into real money during admin payout periods.

2. How do I earn coins?

You earn coins by tapping the big coin, referring friends, watching videos, or engaging in approved tasks. Every action gives you more coins in your wallet.

3. How much is 1 coin worth?

1 coin = ₦0.10 (value may vary based on admin settings).

4. How do I refer others?

After signing up, you’ll get a unique referral link. Share it with friends — each time someone registers through your link, you earn 100 bonus coins.

5. When can I withdraw my coins?

Withdrawals are processed only during official payout times. You will be notified when you’re selected, and the admin will send the money directly to your bank account via Opay.

6. How do I get selected for a payout?

Admin manually selects active users based on activity, referrals, and other criteria. Not everyone is paid at once — but your wallet keeps growing.

7. Is Click Swap real or a scam?

Click Swap is a real, transparent platform manually managed by our team. Coins are tracked in your wallet, and real payouts are made. It’s built for Nigerians, by Nigerians.

8. Where does the payout money come from?

Funds come from advertising revenue, sponsorships, and platform promotions. This is why it's important users stay active and engage with the app to grow earnings for everyone.

9. Can I lose my coins?

No. Your coins remain safe in your account. But abusing the system or using bots may lead to suspension or loss of access.
